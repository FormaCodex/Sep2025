Here at S7SC, we currently offer a handful of courses.

It may be a handful of courses, but these courses are useful in space-based employment and life nonetheless.







MOBILE SUIT PROGRAMMING

The Mobile Suit. A class of machine borne of Zeon heritage, riffed off on by the Earth Federation, and now, a commodity becoming more accessible to the average layman by the day.
These giants of steel, luna titanium and other metals are not all parts and mechanisms, but also most importantly code.
Learn how to code MS joint instructions for different degrees of movement and self-balancing capabilities!







BASIC COMPUTER ANATOMY

The Computer is a machine that has helped usher mankind into space and keeps all who thrive in the stars alive each and every day.
This course will introduce you to the basics of how a Computer works, as well as how to assemble a simple Personal Computer, or PC.
Become enlightened with the knowledge of today and tomorrow now!







SPACECRAFT COMPUTER MAINTENANCE

A Musai-Class, a Shuttle Craft or the Legendary SCV-70 White Base, a Spacecraft is certainly a home for hundreds,
maybe thousands of computer systems, used to take care of nearly everything onboard.
The catch is, however, that all these computers will eventually degrade, lag behind and break down, and someone has to take care of these computers.
Learn to take care of computers today!







SURVIVAL EQUIPMENT MAINTENANCE AND REPAIR

In space, learning to take care of the equipment and machines that keep one alive - be it the seasonal climate control of a space colony,
or the oxygen and waste recycling control of a spacewalking pressure suit - maintaining and repairing these parts of independtly floating colonies is a vital skill needed for those who plan to live long-term or permanently among the cosmos.
Learn these life skills today!
